atha khalu mañjuśrīḥ kumārabhūto bhagavantamevamāhaḥ – tad vadatu bhagavāṃ lokānukampako śāstā

|

evamukte bhagavāṃ mañjuśriyā kumārabhūtena bhagavānetadavocat – śṛṇu mañjuśrīḥ ! ādau tāvad

saphalāni | āśu ca sarvamantraprayogāni siddhiṃ gacchanti | katamaṃ ca tat | bhāṣiṣye'haṃ
śṛṇu kumāra ! ||

ādau tāvad vidyāvrataśīlacaryāsamādānaṃ prathamata eva samādadet | prathamaṃ tāvanmaṇḍalācāryopadeśanasamayamanupraviśet

pūrvasevakṛtavidyaḥ tvadīyamaṇḍalasamanupūrvapraviṣṭaḥ lokajñaḥ vidhijñaḥ samanugrāhakaḥ
kāryāvāṃ vicakṣaṇaḥ śreyasapravṛttaḥ abhīru acchambhinamamaṅkubhūtaḥ dṛḍhavīryaḥ

bhavati ||

sādhakaśca tatsamaḥ nyūno vā kiñcidaṅgaiḥ tādṛśaṃ maṇḍalācāryamabhyarthya prārthayet | icchāmyācāryeṇa

hitacitto dayāvāṃ ||

karmāṇi nānyeṣāmiti vidhireṣā prakīrttitā ||

||
